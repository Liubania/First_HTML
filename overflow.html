Мене звати

Любов Савіних-Пальцева

  • I am Savinykh-Paltseva (nee Savinykh) Liubov Vladimirovna, was born on August 22, 1976 in Odesa in a family of employees.

  • From 1983 to 1993 she studied in high school I-III degree with in-depth study of the German language, which she graduated with a gold medal.

  • While studying at school, I actively studied biology and attended circles of the Regional Station of Young Naturalists (later the Regional Ecological and Naturalistic Center for Young Students) on biology and ecology.

  • She participated in the scientific youth ecological expedition, worked in the sections of the Youth Academy of Sciences "Prometheus", since 1991 she headed its ecological-naturalistic section of the youth presidium.

  • The youth research papers I wrote were awarded with awards at the regional and all-Ukrainian level.

  • From 1993 to 1997 she studied at the environmental department of the Odesa Hydrometeorological Institute, which she graduated with a red diploma.

  • During her studies, she took part in scientific and practical conferences and environmental contests, in 1993 she took part in the international UNESCO-Danube conference in Stuttgart (Germany), was the winner of the All-Ukrainian Olympiad on Ecology in 1996, etc.

  • In addition, during my studies I worked part-time at the Regional Ecological and Naturalistic Center as a leader of environmental circles.

  • After graduating from the Odesa Hydrometeorological Institute from 1997, she worked in the State Administration for Environmental Protection until May 2008 as a chief specialist and head of the department of nature conservation and biological resources.

  • During her work, she participated in international projects TACIS, WWF consultant on issues in accordance with the professional activities.

  • In August 2006, was awarded the Certificate of Honor of the Odesa Regional State Administration.

  • In November 2006, she underwent training in environmental management and conservation management in the Netherlands as part of the TACIS project “Technical Assistance in Lower Dniester Management Planning”, and also conducted training on the development of GIS systems and relevant macrophyte study databases.

  • From 2008 to 2012 she studied in the graduate school of the Odesa I. Mechnikov National University in zoology.

  • Since May 2008 I have been working as a research assistant at the Ukrainian Scientific Center of Ecology of the Sea (UkrSCES) in the Department of Scientific Foundations of Marine Nature Management, Environmental Expertise and Audit.

  • From 20 09 to 2020 she worked as a senior lecturer at the Department of Environmental Law and Control of the Odesa State Ecological University, led a cycle of disciplines on the management of protected natural territories and ecological networks.

  • Since January 2021, Senior Researcher at the Department of Scientific Foundations of Marine Nature Management and Environmental Management.

  • I bring up three daughters.
